Who is Daphne Joy?

Daphne Joy is a Filipino actress and fashion model.

She is best known for her role in movies including Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides, ‘Setup’, and ‘Frankenhood’.

Daphne Joy: Birth Facts, Family, and Childhood

Daphne was born on 8 January 1987 in Olongapo, Philippines. Her nationality is Filipino and her ethnicity is Puerto Rican.

Her mother belongs to the Philippines and her father is of Puerto Rican ancestry. She moved to Los Angeles, California at the age of seven.

Education History

She used to take part in various theater acts, dance groups, and choir during her time at school.

Joy was also a member of the cheerleading team while studying at school.

She began her career as a model at the early age of 17.

Daphne Joy: Professional Life and Career

Initially a model, Daphne Joy also started to appear in numerous television series.

Her most notable roles were in popular series including ‘Criminal Minds’, ‘CSI: Las Vegas, and ‘Curb Your Enthusiasm’.

She later also became a VJ and a dancer for the show called ‘WildnOut’.

After playing relatively small roles in other multiple movies, she made her breakthrough and rose to fame after her part in the movie ‘Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides.

This got her worldwide attention. She portrayed the role of a mermaid in the movie.

She later had a role in the television movie ‘Venice Heat’.

As a model, Joy has made an appearance in numerous magazines in her career.

Some of the magazines that she has been involved with include ‘King Magazine’, ‘Ed Hardy Swim Campaign 2009’, ‘Show Magazine 2011 Calendar’, ‘Performance: Auto and Sound magazine’, ‘Black men magazine’, ‘SMS Headphone Ad Campaign’ and ‘Rukus magazine’.

She also made her appearance in popular music videos like NeYO’s ‘Miss Independent’ and ‘What u Got’.

As an Asian, Joy has been able to keep her mark in the Hollywood Industry.

She has also been a successful figure in the modeling business as well.

Salary and Net Worth
