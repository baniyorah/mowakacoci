What Happened to Farleigh in Saltburn?Who is Farleigh in Saltburn?

Farleigh in Saltburn faces a dark fate as his attempt to sell stolen Palissy plates is exposed. This leads to his expulsion from Felix’s family home, intensifying suspicions about Oliver’s true nature.

Played by Archie Madekwe in the 2023 film “Saltburn,” Farleigh’s expulsion becomes a catalyst for the unfolding tragedy, setting the stage for the mysterious death of Felix and the unsettling aftermath that shrouds Saltburn.

Archie Madekwe, born on February 10, 1995, is an accomplished English actor recognized for his versatile performances in both television and film. He gained attention for his role in the Apple TV+ series “See” (2019–2022) and has notable appearances in “Midsommar” (2019) and the anticipated releases “Gran Turismo” and “Saltburn” (both 2023).

His dedication to his craft led him to the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art (LAMDA), where he left early to star in Ian Rickson’s West End production of “The Goat, or Who Is Sylvia?” alongside Damian Lewis and Sophie Okonedo.

“Saltburn” follows Oliver Quick’s journey from an Oxford outsider to a malevolent puppet master, embedding himself in Felix Catton’s affluent world, where lies and manipulations escalate, ultimately resulting in a chilling finale. The plot intricately weaves betrayal, obsession, and manipulation, leaving a haunting legacy in the once-grand estate.
