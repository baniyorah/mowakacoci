Carrer in Fox 29

Jerrick started his career as a co-host on Fox 29, a news channel from the year 1999 on the show “Good Day Philadelphia.”

Prior, he had co-hosted Fox News Channel’s ‘The Morning Show with Mike and Juliet,’ ‘DaySide’ and ‘Good Day New York.’
