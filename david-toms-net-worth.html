David Toms Wiki Biography

David Wayne Toms, born on January 4, 1967, in Monroe, Louisiana USA, is a professional golfer competing on the PGA Tour. He has secured 13 PGA titles, including the 2001 PGA Championship. Toms began his career in 1989.

As of early 2017, David Toms’ net worth is estimated to be $35 million, earned through his successful career as a professional golfer through prize money and endorsements.

Toms, the son of Thomas Edward Toms, started playing golf at a young age and achieved success, winning the 15-17 Boys’ event at the 1984 Junior World Golf Tournament. He attended Airline High School in Bossier City, Louisiana, and later, he played golf for the team at Louisiana State University in Baton Rouge.

Toms obtained his tour card in 1992 and despite initial struggles, he managed to secure victories and considerable success throughout his career.

He faced ups and downs, but his perseverance paid off, and he achieved numerous victories, including the PGA Championship in 2001, which significantly boosted his net worth at the time. Despite facing performance declines due to injuries, Toms managed to recover and clinch his 13th title at the Crowne Plaza Invitational at Colonial in 2011.
