Albert Walker Mondane Wiki Biography

Albert Walker Mondane, also known as Sauce Walka, was born on the 29th of June 1990, in Houston, Texas USA. He is a rapper and record producer who gained fame as one half of the rap duo Sauce Twinz, alongside Sancho Saucy. He has also collaborated with other renowned rap musicians including Slim Thug and Lil Yachty.

According to authoritative sources, it has been estimated that Walka’s wealth is approximately $200,000, earned through his successful music career which began in 2014.

Sauce Walka’s Net Worth: $200,000

Walka had a challenging childhood, with his mother being a drug addict. He and his brother Dillon faced numerous related problems until their father took them under his wing.

Walka’s musical journey started early, and his collaboration with Sancho led to the formation of the Sauce Twinz. Their mixtapes “In Sauce We Trust” (2014), “Sauce Theft Auto” (2015), and “Don’t Let the Sauce Fool U” (2015) were highly successful, establishing their presence in the rap music scene.

In 2015, Walka released his debut mixtape “Sorry 4 The Sauce”, which included hits such as “Don’t Sell Narcotics No Mo” and “I’m So Saucy”, further boosting his popularity and net worth.

His second mixtape “Sorry 4 The Sauce” was released in the same year and was followed by the third installment in 2016. Throughout the years, he collaborated with renowned artists like 21 Savage, Lil Yachty, Mozzy, and Boosie Badazz, contributing to his wealth.

Like many rappers, Walka has been involved in feuds, notably with rapper Drake. However, this has only propelled his popularity further.

Regarding his personal life, Walka tends to keep it private, leading to minimal information being available about him in the media.
