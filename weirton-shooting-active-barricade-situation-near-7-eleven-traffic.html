Weirton Shooting: Active Barricade Situation Near 7-Eleven Traffic

September 09, 2024
post

Police and SWAT teams in Weirton, West Virginia responded to reports of an active shooter self-barricaded inside a home near the 7-Eleven traffic. According to law enforcement agencies, the male suspect had fled into a house and barricaded himself inside.

Law Enforcement Response

Upon receiving the report, multiple law enforcement vehicles, including two SWAT vehicles, arrived on the scene. Heavily armed police officials were seen surrounding the house where the man was reported to be self-barricaded.

Location and Traffic Disruption

The active incident took place at a residence on Pennsylvania Ave, near the 7-Eleven traffic. Due to the situation, authorities had to redirect traffic in the area.

Ongoing Situation

As of the last report, the situation remained active, with law enforcement still on the scene. The community has been urged to stay clear of the area while the authorities work to resolve the situation.
