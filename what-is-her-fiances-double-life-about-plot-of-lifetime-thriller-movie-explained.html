What is Her Fiances Double Life about? Plot of Lifetime thriller movie explained

August 19, 2024
post

LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA: The upcoming thriller-mystery drama 'Her Fiance's Double Life' (also known as 'Falling For Killer') on Lifetime is about a young assistant attorney named Darcy Young who begins to suspect that her sister's fiance may be a threat to the family.

Comedian, screenwriter, and producer Jay Black wrote the script for 13th Floor Productions in collaboration with Exit 19 Productions, with Lindsay Hartley, best known for her role in 'Passions', serving as director and producing alongside Ron Hanks. Executive producers include Jay Black, Peter Nicoletti, Robert Greco, James Greco, Jack Tinari, and Bill Spadea.

What is the plot of ‘Her Fiance’s Double Life’?

Darcy Young, an assistant district attorney, is invited to her parent's country home to meet her sister's fiance, Thomas Schure. Darcy Young initially becomes skeptical of the duo's whirlwind romance, which makes her anxious, and she begins to keep an eye on Schure's behavior. With time, Thomas Schure's behavior becomes much more unpredictable, and Young suspects that he is not who he claims to be and is a danger to her family.

'Her Fiance's Double Life' will be available to stream on Lifetime at 8 pm ET on April 22, 2023.
