Many of us have to think deep when talking about Bruno Mars relationship status. That is because the singer keeps his personal life very private. In fact, Mars is rumored to be in an affair with many celebrities as per online tabloids. But you will be surprised to know that the "Grenade" singer is romantically involved with one woman for almost eight years now. Who is the lucky lady? Let's know more about her.

Bruno Mars is dating Jessica Caban since 2011

Mars met Jessica for the first time in 2011, and since then the couple is inseparable. His relationship is on the smooth ride just like his successful career. So, you might be wondering who is Jessica Caban? 

Well, she is a pretty successful model who also won the reality TV Show called Model Latina. As a winner, she collected a $10,000 contract with a company called Q management. Besides that, Caban currently is the owner of her own swimwear brand called J. Marie Swimwear.

Caban and Mars started dating each other since 2011 after they first locked eyes with each other in a New York City restaurant. 

Are they getting Married?

The pair is deeply in love. Don't believe it? Just check out Jessica's Instagram profile. 

The pair is seen together in so many cute pics cozying up with each other. Bruno is not someone who publicizes his affairs so the couple is keeping their romance with each other as possible.

Eight years now, are they thinking of getting married? Apparently not, as per sources, the duo is currently living together but not pronounced husband and wife.

In 2017, the Evening Standard reported about Mars living together with his lover in his mansion in the Hollywood Hills. In January 2017, when talking about his late mom in an interview, 24K Magic singer also discussed Jessica and what he thinks about the relationship? He said,

"The woman who taught you to love, showed you what a woman is supposed to be. When it comes to relationships, it's all in. And that's what keeps that fire going".

Not just that, in an interview with Rolling Stone 2016 , the singer commented on him tying the knot with Caban. He said,

"Jesus! she's my best friend. My rock. What's wrong with that? We're just happy".

It looks like the couple is not thinking of exchanging wedding vows anytime soon.

Jessica Caban expecting a Child?

According to some online tabloids, Jessica and Bruno are expecting a child together. But that's not true at all. The couple is very focused on their career and on each other. They are not even thinking about having a child as of now.

But that doesn't mean, Bruno Mars, doesn't want a child. He was born into a big family with a brother and four sisters. Mars grew up in a musical family as his father was a singer and his mother was a dancer. 

As of now, his brother and sisters are all musicians and are thriving in their career. Just like his family, Bruno hopes to have a big family of his own children as well. Getting inspired by his late mother, he will be a great father in the future but for now, he and his lover are not expecting a child.
