Billionaire Michael Rubin Speaks Out About Kim Kardashian and Tom Brady Dating Speculations

September 08, 2024
post

Kim Kardashian and Tom Brady’s alleged dating rumors have been addressed by US billionaire businessman Michael Rubin, who hosted the Hamptons white party where the two superstars crossed paths.

The CEO of Fanatics discussed Kim and Brady in an interview with Entertainment Tonight while attending the Shawn Carter Foundation in New York City.

“Honestly, they’re just friends,” said Rubin, 50, regarding the two celebrities’ interaction at the July 4 event. “It’s just the crazy rumors that get out there.”

Rubin mentioned spending a significant amount of time with the retired NFL star during the Independence Day celebration.

“Tom was with me a ton of the night and we were having fun, and Tom just doesn’t go out that much,” Rubin said, describing the three-time NFL MVP’s presence at the party as “a rare sighting.”

Kardashian revealed on social media that she took 11 shots at the summer bash, with Rubin noting that “Kim doesn’t drink much.”

He added, “So I think Kim’s 10 or 12 shots she had, [and] Tom, you know, being fun, it just leads to rumors. We always want to laugh about it.”

The dating rumors sparked when the reality star and retired quarterback were seen engrossed in conversation at the event, which attracted numerous A-list celebrities.

The outlet DeuxMoi obtained a photo of Kardashian and Brady engaging in a chat.
