Who is Ben Richardson?

Ben Richardson is a renowned English cinematographer and television producer.

He is best known for his works in the films like Beasts of the Southern Wils (2012), The Fault in Our Stars (2014), Wind River (2017), and Mare of Easttown (2021).

What is the full name of Ben Richardson?

Ben was born in England, the United Kingdom on September 21, 1975.

His full name is Benjamin T. Richardson.

Ben hasn’t yet disclosed his parents and other family member’s identity in the media.

Talking about his academic background, he attended Bournemouth School.

For further studies, Ben Richardson joined Royal Holloway, the University of London’s media arts degree program.

Further, Ben Richardson holds a British nationality and he belongs to the Caucasian ethnicity.

Ben Richardson: Professional Life, Career

  • In 2004, Ben Richardson met director Benh Zeitlin in Prague with whom he collaborated on the 2008 short film, Glory at Sea.
  • Ben debuted in Hollywood as a cinematographer for the film, Beasts of the Southern Wild. The film stars Quvenzhane Wallis and Dwight Henry.
  • He then worked with the director Joe Swanberg in the 2013 comedy-drama film, Drinking Buddies.
  • Richardson also worked in television series like The Chi (2018), Yellowstone (2018), and Mare of Easttown (2021).
  • Similarly, Ben made a big break after he worked as the cinematographer of the film adaptation of John Green’s The Fault in Our Stars.
  • Ben Richardson was also recognized for his worth in the animated stop-motion film Seed, which got the Best Animated Short Film at the Sundance Film Festival.
